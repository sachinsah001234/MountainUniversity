Mountain University, Shimla

WELCOME TO INDIA'S NO.1 UNIVERSITY

India's one of the best University. Mountain University, Shimla, was established on 1st April 1985 by the Legislature of the State of Himachal Pradesh as the first Technological University in the country. It has been in the forefront for the past many years in providing quality technological education of relevance in the State of Andhra Pradesh. After 36 years of existence it was restructured into 4 different Universities by the Government. and this new University has been designated as Mountain University(MU).
